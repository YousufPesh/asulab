Key Architecture Principles

Single Source of Truth

Course content is the only source. No hallucination outside provided materials.

Academic Integrity

No grading capabilities. Designed to assist learning, not complete assignments.

Privacy First

Chat history is anonymized for analytics. FERPA compliant by design.

Instructor Control

Faculty retain full control over what content is ingested and how the agent behaves.
